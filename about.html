About Wealth Builder Tools

Wealth Builder Tools is a collection of free, browser-based financial calculators designed to help everyday people understand their money, make smarter investing decisions, and build long-term wealth.

Every tool on this site runs entirely in your browser. No account required. No data stored. No email needed. Just open a calculator, enter your numbers, and get instant, accurate results.

Why I Built This

Personal finance is one of the most important life skills most people are never taught. I built these tools because I wanted straightforward, no-nonsense calculators that answer the questions I actually had — things like "how much does waiting to invest really cost me?" and "what would I need to put away each month to hit $1 million by 40?"

Most financial calculators online are either buried behind paywalls, cluttered with ads, or require you to hand over your email address. These are free, clean, and always will be.

About the Calculations

All calculators use standard, widely accepted financial formulas. The compound interest and investment growth tools use industry-standard compounding math. The real estate calculator uses cap rate, cash-on-cash return, and gross rent multiplier formulas consistent with professional real estate analysis.

Results are estimates for educational purposes. They do not account for taxes, inflation, investment risk, or your specific financial situation. See the Disclaimer for full details.
