Contact Us

Have a question about one of the calculators? Found a bug? Want to suggest a new tool? We'd love to hear from you.

The best way to reach us is by email. We typically respond within 1–2 business days.

✉️ Email: james@9dimesproject.com
🌐 Web: 9dimesproject.com

What to Include

To help us respond quickly, please include:

— The name of the calculator or page you're asking about
— A brief description of your question or the issue you encountered
— Your browser and device type if you're reporting a bug (e.g., Chrome on iPhone)

Feature Requests

We're always looking to add new tools. If there's a financial calculator you wish existed — budgeting, investing, real estate, debt payoff, or anything else — let us know. The best tools on this site have come from exactly these kinds of suggestions.
